Notes
Backstory

Nocere was born into a rural family, two older brothers soon be to joined by a younger sister. Even though it was a hard farm life, his family cared a great deal for each other.

 

Unlike a lot of villages who had fallen on hard times, his village was renowned for their generosity. Unfortunately, their good nature did hot help against a force of nomadic hobgoblins who came to plunder and pillage, who correctly determined that it was an easy target.

 

At five years of age, at night, and without warning, hobgoblins swarmed upon the village. Nocere's farm being in the path of the hobgoblins, got hit quickly. The hobgoblins not known for being subtle, broke down the door. His father was startled awake, reached under his bed, and as they entered his bed room, barely had enough time to pull a dagger out, before he was decapitated.

 

The children when found by the hobgoblins, who were already wide eyed from the noises they had heard, were further alarmed by the sight of the intruders. The children were dragged outside screaming and crying.

 

One of the hobgoblins, who appeared more decorated than the others, stood over and examined the children. He spoke words which Nocere did not understand, pointing to him and his sister, and then waved his hand and walked way towards the town.

 

Their hands were bound. An hobgoblin shouted words turning them around to face their two older brothers. They shrieked when they saw to hobgoblins coming them with their knives drawn, and the sight of his brothers terrified expressions as the knife was placed at their throats and the subsequent red blood which spilled forth would forever haunt his dreams.

 

Nocere shook in fear, they were hoisted up, and placed on a worg. He never learned what happened to his mother, which he didn't see again.

 

That long terrible night faded into a new day, when his sister too was taken away. Nocere quickly realized what was happening. He was owned by the hobgoblins, passed between them. He was to be trained to always obey orders, and treated as a thing to ridiculed and tortured by younger hobgoblins. Over time he learned their language as they vigorously taught it to him in the most strenuous of ways.

 

In regards to a small bit of enjoy he had, was that because the hobgoblin encampments were often overrun by mice, he managed at times to befriend a few of them, the first of which, came to a grizzly fate by his masters hands. However, he developed quite a talent for it for the course of the years.

 

He learned to sneak in shadows, avoiding the constant threat of those who would want to show of their dominance to other hobgoblins, or who simply wanted to have "fun."

 

A night came when he was to be placed in a worg cage, not enough room to stand up in, is when he decided to take a chance. The worgs were taken, and the men left for another pillage, he managed to free himself due to a pitiful lock, subsequently he sneaked away in the night. He ran as far as he could without sleep or eating, following a river to hide his tracks.

 

He came to a town of humans and other races. He at first begged for money, but that money was short lived, as he was easily taken advantage of, which taught him a valuable "lesson."

 

The town he had come to seek shelter, was constantly reminding him of his past in that it also had its share of slaves owned by the wealthy. He felt anger and empathy, and it was no longer was just an "hobgoblin" thing. Disgust began to shape in regards to those abused their power, and his restraint to not act on it became ever more so difficult to resist.

 

He was drawn to the areas which never saw the light of day, to make use of his talents he developed. In time, he learned how to live on the street, still trying to find a new family, people who he could trust...
Pet Mouse is named marshmellow.
Physical Due to his many years of enslavement and harsh treatment, he has many scars all over his body, most hidden by his cloak.
